Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2025

Welcome to Solenne Flashcards. This policy explains our common sense privacy rules.

1. Signing In (Optional)

You do not need to log in to use Solenne Flashcards. If you use a Gemini API key locally without logging in, zero data is sent to our Solenne servers.

If you choose to log in, we offer Google OAuth and Sign in with Apple. We never store your personal data, like your name or email, from these services.

We only use a private, unique number (your User ID) to identify your account.

2. The Only Data We Keep

We only store two types of data:

  • Your private User ID.
  • The flashcards you create (the content itself).

3. How We Use Your Flashcards

The content of the flashcards you generate may be used to train and improve AI models. This is specifically for Google Gemini models and is at Google's discretion. Otherwise we don't use your flashcard content in any way other than displaying them to you in the app or website.

4. Deleting Your Data

You have total control. You can delete your account inside the app settings.

Deleting your account immediately and permanently removes your User ID and all your flashcards.

This action cannot be undone.

5. Contact Us

For any questions about this policy, please email help@solenne.ai.
